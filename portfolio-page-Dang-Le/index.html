About Me

I recently graduate from Arcada University Of Applied Sciences in Helsinki, Finland. My degree program is called Materials Processing Technology. My study focuses on designing products based on the functionality of the materials, and different manufacturing processes like injection molding and prepreg laminating. I gained most of my experience working in projects. The projects range from designing 3D object, making process analyses to manufacturing actual products.

My biggest project so far is also my thesis topic: where I have to understand the principle and design a machine to improve the quality of Prepreg composite made from the lab. The result is a compact, easy to assemble and affordable machine made from laser-cut MDF material, which is also highly rated by my mentor and colleagues.
