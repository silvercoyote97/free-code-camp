About Me

I recently graduate from Arcada University Of Applied Sciences in Helsinki, Finland. My degree program is called Materials Processing Technology. My study focuses on designing products based on the functionality of the materials, and different manufacturing processes like injection molding and prepreg laminating. I gained most of my experience working in projects. The projects range from designing 3D object, making process analyses to manufacturing actual products.

My biggest project so far is also my thesis topic: where I have to understand the principle and design a machine to improve the quality of Prepreg composite made from the lab. The result is a compact, easy to assemble and affordable machine made from laser-cut MDF material, which is also highly rated by my mentor and colleagues.

My Skill

Over the time that I have in Arcada University, I got a chance to understand the industrial process of making products, and took part in two of the most essential: the design process and manufacturing process. Understanding the strong connection between software and hardware, I have been trying and implementing new software into my workflow, while continuously improving my skill with the commonly-known software that I have been using.

Software

As an engineer, it is essential to train my mind to visualize every objects in 2D or 3D. I mainly used SolidWorks and AutoCAD to design the products, while Fusion360 and Blender are occasionally used in prototyping or for simpler shapes. Then some analyses can be done by either Mastercam or Moldflow, depend on the machinery used to make the parts. This step can provide the baseline parameters to experiment while running the machines.
